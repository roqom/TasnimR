Hello World

June 15, 2025

Welcome to my blog!

If you’re among the first people reading this, you’ll notice the site is still bare and minimal. It’s running on the default WordPress theme — Twenty Twenty-Five — because I just created and launched it.

This blog is my personal public space online, where I plan to explore three things: 

1. Writing

I want to sharpen my writing skills, especially for the online world. I’ll focus more on persuasive writing — not necessarily about writing itself, but also using the skill to explore topics that interest me and help me grow as a person.

2. Building

I plan to build systems — in business and in life. To me, a system is a method for solving problems efficiently and making life smoother. My goal is peace of mind through better systems.

3. Selling

Eventually, I hope to earn income beyond my 9-to-5 job. So, I’ll explore how to sell something meaningful using what I learn from writing and building — in other words, entrepreneurship. Lol.

Starting something new always brings a rush of excitement and wanting to do many things— and that’s exactly where I am right now. I’m still figuring things out. But I believe that if I keep showing up and writing consistently, I'll find what to focus on, InsyaAllah. 

As I continue, I’ll add more features or update the design if needed. But for now, I enjoy this minimalist look and feel.

Oh, one more thing — if you’re on Facebook, feel free to add me as friend or follow me for updates. Here's my profile: https://www.facebook.com/roq0m.

Thanks for being here 😊
